Our Mission

Honest work.
Honest people.

Solid Grain was built on one idea: do the job properly, every time. No shortcuts, no cheap materials, no disappearing acts.

Who We Are

A small team that cares.

We're a London-based timber fencing and outdoor work contractor. We started because we saw too many fencing companies cutting corners — cheap timber, rushed jobs, no clean-up.

Solid Grain is different. We use pressure-treated timber on every job, we turn up when we say we will, and we leave the site cleaner than we found it. Every job gets the same attention, whether it's a three-panel repair or a full commercial boundary.

We're based in Ruislip and cover all of London and the surrounding areas. We work with homeowners, landlords, property developers and housing associations.
